Title: HR Analytics Dashboard.

	The HR Analytics Dashboard is a comprehensive data visualization project developed using Power BI, a powerful business intelligence tool. This project aims to provide HR professionals and decision-makers with an interactive and informative dashboard that presents key HR metrics and insights for effective workforce management and decision-making.

The main objectives of the HR Analytics Dashboard project include:

Data Integration: The project involves integrating data from various HR systems and sources such as employee records, performance management systems, recruitment databases, training and development platforms, and employee survey data.

Data Modeling and Transformation: Raw HR data is transformed and modeled to create a structured and organized data model suitable for analysis. This may involve data cleansing, data merging, and creating relationships between different HR data sources.

Visualizations and Interactive Elements: The dashboard utilizes a variety of visualizations such as charts, graphs, heat maps, and tables to present HR analytics in a visually appealing and easy-to-understand manner. Interactive elements such as slicers, filters, and drill-through functionalities allow users to explore the data and gain deeper insights.

Workforce Demographics: This section provides visual representations of employee demographics, including age distribution, gender diversity, and ethnic composition.

Employee Performance and Engagement: This section highlights key performance indicators (KPIs) related to employee performance, productivity, and engagement. 

The HR Analytics Dashboard provides HR professionals and decision-makers with a centralized platform to analyze and understand critical HR metrics, uncover insights, and make informed decisions to enhance workforce management, engagement, and organizational success.
